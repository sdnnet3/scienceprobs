Okay, so what is all this?

Our goal is to offer study guides, tips and tricks to help get you through many university level classes. We are well underway recording videos and designing tools to help make it through university. We aren't here to just give the solutions to a problem, but develop the skills that go into really understanding the material.

A blip... blurb? Heck, here's a little bit about me.

Who's that guy? Well, it's me. Hello, I'm Clayton, creator of Science Problems. I've finally made the jump to... well, jump in on this head first. That sounds painful, I know. I'm a Biochemist here in San Diego, born and raised. I don't come from a family of Scientists, so there was a steep learning curve on my end. I didn't give up, and neither should you.

About Science Problems

Science Probs? What heck? Well, mostly remembering the many frustrating hours spent in the commuter lounge on campus, late nights, endless youtube searches for information that was not delivered very well, and many other things along the way, I decided to work on delivering something that everyone here could use.
